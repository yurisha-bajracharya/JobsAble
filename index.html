About Us


JobsAble is an organization for people who are differently abled. This is your corner!

We work with the moto: Sharing is Caring!
We, at JobsAble, understand how difficult it can be for people to get employed especially when one is differently abled. So here is the solution! You'll get all the necessary information about available jobs, requirements for you. You can even personalize your needs.
Happy Working!

Our Activities

Asakta Janachetana Abhiyan

We, the members at JobsAble, conducted an awareness campaign to inform about the existing opportunites for differently abled people in Nepal.

Details

Inter-school Poetry Competition

JobsAble organized a school level poetry competition with the help of the school itself on the topic "Differently Abled: More abled" where in students recited poems out of their creativity for differently abled people.

Details

Local Job Knowledge

We organized a program called 'Local Job Knowledge' for the people at rural areas to get informed about the jobs available locally for them.
